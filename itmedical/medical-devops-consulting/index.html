  • Fast Delivery of Reliable Software

  • Rapid Response to Change
    By using our serviceы, healthcare organisations can quickly adapt to changes through rapid software updates and iterations, ensuring compliance and high standards of care.
  • Flexibility and Scalability
    Our DevOps service provides the scalability and flexibility to adjust resources in line with fluctuating demands or changes in market and regulatory environment.

Infrastructure-as-code

Through our DevOps services, we can integrate infrastructure management strategies with code deployment practices. Our goal is to diminish the manual labour involved while also ensuring consistency across your infrastructure. We specialise in automating tasks and developing bespoke solutions to integrate Infrastructure-as-code into your existing workflows.

  • App & middleware management tools

    Our skilled team can design, develop, and deliver customised tools for managing application servers and code-based applications tailored to your specific needs.

  • Environment & deployment tools

  • Generic & multi-functional tools

Regulatory and Compliance

Our certification portfolio now includes CISSP, PCI, FIPS 140-2, ISO, GDPR, and HIPAA compliance, in addition to alignment with HL7 International standards.
