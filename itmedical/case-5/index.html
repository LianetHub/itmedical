Challenge

Streamlining the Patient Journey with a Comprehensive SaaS Telehealth Platform that Integrates with EHRs and PMSs

The idea behind the platform was to streamline the patient journey and introduce new online services to patients. The objective was to improve conversion rates from digital marketing campaigns, increase patient retention, and boost revenue. The platform needed to include an online booking tool, a patient portal with relevant information, and two mobile applications supporting video calls, text chat, and file exchange.

  1. Improving Conversion Rates: The customer aimed to enhance conversion rates from digital marketing campaigns. To achieve this goal, it was necessary to create a user-friendly platform that facilitates easy online booking and patient interaction.
  2. Enhancing Patient Retention: To increase patient retention, the platform needed to offer comprehensive services that keep patients engaged and satisfied with their care experience.
  3. Revenue Growth: By streamlining administrative tasks and improving service delivery, the platform aimed to boost overall revenue for healthcare providers.
  4. Comprehensive Online Services: The platform required features like online booking, appointment reminders, cancellations, video consultations, feedback forms, and patient reviews to provide a complete digital healthcare experience.
  5. Integration with Existing Systems: To be effective, the new platform had to integrate seamlessly with major Practice Management Systems (PMS) and Electronic Health Records (EHR) solutions available in the US market.
  6. Minimizing Administrative Burden: The platform needed to automate routine administrative tasks to reduce the workload on healthcare providers’ administrative staff.

Given these challenges, the task was to develop a SaaS telehealth platform that would address these specific needs, enhance patient engagement, and streamline the patient journey from start to finish.

Solution

Comprehensive Cross-Platform SaaS Telehealth Solution: Enhancing Patient Engagement and Streamlining Administration

The IT Medical team developed a comprehensive cross-platform solution with an external API that can be integrated with major PMS and EHR solutions in the US market. The SaaS platform automates the entire patient journey, from online booking, appointment reminders, and cancellations to online consultations, feedback forms, and patient reviews.

  1. Online Booking and Patient Portal: The platform includes an online booking tool and a patient portal that provides relevant information, making it easy for patients to schedule and manage their appointments.
  2. Mobile Applications: Two mobile applications (iOS and Android) support video calls, text chat, and file exchange, enabling secure and convenient communication between patients and healthcare providers.
  3. Comprehensive CMS System: To minimize routine admin tasks, a comprehensive Content Management System (CMS) was developed. It handles day-to-day activities of healthcare providers’ admin staff, including managing queues, appointments, invoicing, and more.
  4. HIPAA-Compliant Doctors Portal: For remote visits, a HIPAA-compliant doctors portal was developed, ensuring secure communication between patients and doctors.
  5. Integration Capabilities: The platform’s external API allows for seamless integration with major PMS and EHR solutions, ensuring that all patient data is synchronized and accessible across systems.

Outcome

Transforming Healthcare Delivery with a Comprehensive SaaS Telehealth Platform

In eight months, the IT Medical team developed, tested, and launched an online booking widget, a cloud-based admin system, and web-based patient and doctor portals with iOS and Android mobile apps. An additional two months were taken to fully launch the mobile applications. As a result, over 30% of patients switched to online booking, significantly reducing the workload on the admin team.

The solution is currently used by over 100 small to mid-sized healthcare providers in the US, with 800+ healthcare employees managing patients and conducting remote visits each month. Our team continues to improve the platform by adding new features and working closely with the client’s marketing team to achieve even better conversion rates and Lifetime Value (LTV).

The implementation of our SaaS telehealth platform has:

  • Enhanced Patient Engagement: The user-friendly online booking and patient portal keep patients engaged and satisfied with their care experience.
  • Streamlined Administration: The automated admin tasks significantly reduce the workload on healthcare providers’ administrative staff, improving overall efficiency.
  • Improved Conversion Rates: The platform’s comprehensive features and seamless integration with digital marketing efforts have increased conversion rates.
  • Scalable and Flexible Solution: The platform is continuously improved and adapted to meet the evolving needs of healthcare providers, ensuring it remains a valuable tool in digital healthcare delivery.

FAQ

  1. What are the key benefits of a custom SaaS telehealth platform compared to a generic system?

    A custom SaaS telehealth platform is tailored to meet the specific needs of healthcare providers, ensuring better alignment with unique workflows. It enhances user experience, integrates seamlessly with other healthcare technologies, and ensures robust data management, ultimately improving efficiency and patient care.

  2. How does integrating an online booking tool into a telehealth platform benefit healthcare providers and patients?

    Integrating an online booking tool allows patients to easily schedule and manage their appointments, reducing the workload on administrative staff and improving overall patient satisfaction. This convenience enhances patient engagement and retention.

  3. What challenges do generic telehealth systems pose for healthcare providers?

    Generic telehealth systems often fail to provide comprehensive patient journey management, lack seamless integration with existing systems, and do not offer automated admin task management. These shortcomings can lead to inefficiencies, fragmented patient care, and increased administrative burden.

  4. How does a custom SaaS telehealth platform ensure compliance with healthcare regulations?

    A custom SaaS telehealth platform is designed with built-in compliance features that adhere to healthcare regulations such as HIPAA. It includes security measures to protect patient data and ensures all regulatory standards are consistently met, reducing the risk of data breaches and legal issues.

  5. Can a custom SaaS telehealth platform scale with the growth of a healthcare provider?

    Yes, a custom SaaS telehealth platform is designed to be scalable and flexible. It can evolve with the growing needs of the healthcare provider, supporting the addition of new features and integrations with other healthcare services, ensuring continuous improvement and adaptability.
