Our certificates

At IT Medical, we prioritize high standards and quality in our healthcare technology solutions. Our certifications showcase our commitment to excellence and trustworthiness in the industry.

  • Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine
    DICOM is an international standard for managing medical imaging information and ensuring interoperability among imaging systems. Compliance allows devices to work together enhancing diagnosis accuracy, treatment effectiveness, and workflow efficiency in healthcare settings.
  • Health Level Seven International
    HL7 International sets standards for the exchange and integration of electronic health information to improve interoperability. Compliance ensures smooth information exchange across healthcare systems, enhancing care coordination, reducing errors, and improving outcomes.
  • International Organisation for Standardisation
    ISO/IEC 62304 defines life cycle requirements for medical device software, focusing on safety and reliability. Compliance ensures safe software design and maintenance, preventing failures that could harm patients and helping manufacturers meet regulatory standards for approval.
  • International Organisation for Standardisation
    ISO/IEC 27001:2022 is a standard for managing information security, establishing and improving an information security management system (ISMS). Compliance protects sensitive health information from breaches, maintaining data confidentiality, integrity, and availability in healthcare.
  • Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act
    HIPAA is a US law that sets privacy standards to protect patients' medical records and health information. Compliance is mandatory for US healthcare providers, ensuring the protection of patient data from unauthorized access and breaches, maintaining patient trust and avoiding penalties.
  • Food and Drug
    Administration
    FDA 21 CFR Part 11 sets criteria for electronic records and signatures to be considered trustworthy and reliable. Compliance is crucial for medical and pharmaceutical organizations using electronic records, ensuring security, accuracy, facilitating regulatory inspections, and maintaining data integrity.
  • Cyber Essentials
    Cyber Essentials is a UK scheme providing basic security controls to protect against common cyber attacks. Compliance demonstrates that an organization has taken essential steps to safeguard against cyber threats, protecting patient data and maintaining the integrity of healthcare services.
