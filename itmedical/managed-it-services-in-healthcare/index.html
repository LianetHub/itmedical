Managed IT Services

We offer a fully comprehensive suite of IT management services, from robust IT support to advanced cloud services and data management. Our managed IT services are designed to relieve the IT burden from healthcare organisations, enabling them to focus on enhancing patient care.

  • Custom Software Management

    Through our software management service, IT Medical ’s experienced team ensures that healthcare software retains high performance while safeguarding security and enhancing the user experience.

  • Infrastructure Management

    Infrastructure management involves maintaining networks, storage systems, devices, and system administration, ensuring all components are functional, secure, and unsusceptible to errors or breaches.

  • Risk Evaluation
    & Mitigation

    Risk mitigation involves a strategic approach to tackle a variety of infrastructure challenges, implementing preventative strategies with the aim of reducing future risks that can affect all levels of healthcare organisations.

Why Use IT Medical’s Managed IT Services?

By outsourcing IT management to a specialized provider like IT Medical, healthcare organizations can focus more time and resources on delivering quality patient care.

  • Risk Mitigation
    ITmed’s managed IT services mitigate risks by resolving technological or security issues and strategically applying preventative measures. This includes disaster recovery plans, restoring network connectivity, stress testing, and rapidly responding to existing or potential future security issues.  
  • Monitoring, Feedback, and Optimization
    Through 24/7 monitoring, management, and support, our specialized managed IT services can rapidly identify and resolve issues before they lead to system failures or downtime. This proactive approach ensures that healthcare operations remain uninterrupted for continuous patient care.
  • Technological and User-Focused Approach
    Our technology- and user-focused approach means that, while we strive to ensure technologies are always in optimal condition, we also focus on enhancing the user experience. This ensures digital solutions can be seamlessly integrated into user’s routines, for optimal engagement.

Regulatory and Compliance

In maintaining global compliance, we have fortified our capacity to produce software conforming to international healthcare norms such as HIPAA, IEC 62304, DICOM, ISO 13485, HL7, and FDA 21. Our certification portfolio now includes CISSP, PCI, FIPS 140-2, ISO, GDPR, and HIPAA compliance, in addition to alignment with HL7 International standards.

360° IT Infrastructure Management

Our comprehensive infrastructure management services help healthcare organisations and care providers to manage and maintain the integrity and security of their IT infrastructure, regardless of its size or complexity.

  • Network management

    Our skilled team oversees networks, verifying that their structures are properly designed and required resources are allocated efficiently.

  • Storage management

    We manage data centres and independent storage solutions, focusing on safeguarding data and distributing resources effectively.

  • System administration

    We manage IT resources, including databases, physical and virtual servers, and active directories, to ensure they remain accessible and secure.

  • Device management

    We continuously monitor and assess the security of company devices using bespoke protocols and advanced remote access tools & technologies.

Cloud Infrastructure Management

We offer comprehensive management services for various cloud configurations, including public, private, hybrid, and multi-cloud setups, using an approach tailored to the specific requirements of healthcare organisations.

Client's Infrastructure
We can oversee cloud deployments using your existing infrastructure. We can manage every step, including hardware and software components and deployment, regularly updating the client and providing ongoing support.
IT Medical's Infrastructure
We can also offer our own infrastructure in order to help organisations reduce infrastructure-related expenses. Again, we can manage every step of the process and guarantee the environment’s availability and security.
Bespoke Solutions
Finally, we can develop tailor-made cloud management solutions, including advanced features for effective monitoring and analysis of cloud environments, supervision of user activities, and prevention of threats.

Our Managed IT Services in Action

  1. Evaluation
    First, we evaluate infrastructure and software to understand their current state and management requirements.
  2. Scope
    Following evaluation, we collaborate with the client to develop a plan for the project’s scope, duration, and cost.
  3. Team selection
    Once aligned on the project’s scope, IT Medical assembles an expert team of highly skilled professionals.
  4. Agreement
    Together with the client, we review the project plan and, once both parties are happy, sign an agreement.
  5. Process
    Once a plan is in place and an agreement signed, we provide managed IT services in line with our agreement.
