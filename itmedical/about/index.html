Your Trusted Partner

Our ultimate goal is to bring value to your business by delivering quality services with lower investments. We match our processes with your preferences so that you feel confident and secure outsourcing work to us.

  • To make outsourcing engagements transparent for clients and developers, we leverage planning, reporting, and change management tools. This approach allows you to control the project timeline and budget.
  • Based on your unique project objectives, we propose the most efficient cooperation model and development methodology. We assemble a team of experts with relevant experience, ensuring a tailored approach that meets your specific needs.
  • With headquarters in the USA and representative offices in Europe and Asia, we can easily adapt to your timetable. Regardless of location, we guarantee brisk responsiveness through multiple communication channels.
  • Our approach combines the best aspects of Lean and Scrum to foster on‑demand scalability and agility in our healthcare software development. We are ready to adapt development processes to your schedule and requirement shifts at any project stage while reducing the cost of change.
  • Our comprehensive risk management policy ensures safe and predictable relationships by providing a set of practices for identifying and eliminating software risks before they threaten successful software operations or become a significant source of expensive rework.
