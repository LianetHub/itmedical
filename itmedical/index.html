Regulatory Compliance

We ensure global compliance by developing software that meets international healthcare standards, including HIPAA, IEC 62304, DICOM, Cyber Essentials, ISO 13485, HL7, and FDA 21. Our expertise extends to certifications such as CISSP, PCI, FIPS 140-2, ISO, GDPR, and HIPAA, along with adherence to HL7 International standards.
