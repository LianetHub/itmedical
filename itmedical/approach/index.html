IT Medical Approach

At IT Medical, our dedicated teams are committed to transforming healthcare operations and improving patient care with innovative digital health solutions.

Understanding Your Needs

  • Deep Industry Knowledge

    With in-depth knowledge of healthcare ecosystems, we develop solutions that resolve many of the prevalent issues in the field. Our digital health solutions offer support in everything from regulatory compliance to workflow optimization. They are meticulously planned and designed to meet the latest industry needs.

  • Tailored Solutions

    Our healthcare solutions are tailored to meet the specific needs and requirements of our clients. From integrating innovative digital technologies to revamping outdated systems, we put efficiency, security, interoperability, and user-friendliness above everything else.

How We Work

  1. 1 Discovery process

    We start by refining your initial concept through detailed interviews and feedback sessions, ensuring all requirements are clearly defined.

  2. 2 Screens & flow

    Next, we design key layouts and workflows, shaping 80% of the system’s visual and functional design to align with your needs.

  3. 3 Demonstration

    We then present a live demo, allowing you to experience the system firsthand. Once approved, the design is finalized, and we move forward with development.

  4. 4 Development

    Our team focuses on building the solution while keeping communication open for any needed clarifications. This is where your vision becomes a reality.

  5. 5 Testing and Quality Assurance

    Before launch, we rigorously test the system to ensure high-quality performance, security, and compliance.

  6. 6 Implementation and Support

    Finally, we seamlessly integrate the system into your operations and provide ongoing support for a smooth transition and continuous optimization.

Collaborative Approach

    Collaboration is at the heart of our approach, driven by the specific needs of our clients. To ensure these needs are met at every stage, we work closely with our clients throughout the entire project lifecycle. We value transparency, efficiency, and trust.

  • Cross-Functional Teams

  • Dedicated Teams

    Our dedicated teams are experts in creating tailored solutions for complex projects. With deep expertise in software and healthcare, we assemble Smart Teams to deliver exceptional results and address our clients' specific needs.

Our top priority is protecting patient data. We implement strict security measures to prevent data breaches, safeguard sensitive information, and maintain client trust.

What We Offer

  1. Fixed price

    We offer fixed-price models for projects with clear scope and requirements. Our well-planned milestones guarantee cost certainty and full transparency from day one.

  2. Time & materials

    Our time and materials model is designed to meet your evolving needs, billing only for the work done and resources used, offering flexibility without sacrificing control.

  3. Dedicated team

    Our dedicated Smart Teams, led by top app developers, are here to execute your project seamlessly. We bring your vision to life quickly and precisely, ensuring seamless collaboration throughout.

  4. Team augmentation

    Our distributed team model ensures a smooth, collaborative workflow with your in-house team. We make sure everyone stays on the same page and the project moves forward without a hitch.

  5. Service for Equity

    We offer flexible service-for-equity models, assessing your product’s scalability potential. Partner with us for ongoing support and expert guidance, ensuring your product scales seamlessly.
