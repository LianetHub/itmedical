Services

We are redefining the healthcare experience by integrating sophisticated software solutions that streamline and enhance every aspect of patient care and healthcare provider operations. From facilitating remote consultations to optimizing patient data management, our mission is to usher in the future of healthcare today.

  • Software Engineering Transforming healthcare through innovative and dedicated software engineering services, we provide reliable solutions.
  • Application Services Our team excels in creating custom-built applications designed to meet the specific demands of healthcare settings.
  • Digital Product Consulting We provide comprehensive healthcare IT solutions, including software management, integration, infrastructure, and risk mitigation.
  • DevOps Services Our DevOps services for the healthcare sector bridge the gap between development and operations with automated software delivery.
  • Quality Assurance and Testing At ITmed, we deliver quality assurance and testing services to ensure software is functional, user-centric, compliant, and robust.
  • Software Support IT Medical ensures seamless integration, enhancing patient care, data management, and operational efficiency.
  • Dedicated Teams Our skilled experts offer flexible solutions for managing complex healthcare projects and operational challenges.

Regulatory Compliance

We ensure global compliance by developing software that meets international healthcare standards, including HIPAA, IEC 62304, DICOM, Cyber Essentials, ISO 13485, HL7, and FDA 21.
