Maintenance & Support

IT Medical recognizes the role software systems play in healthcare organizations. We also understand the impact software interruptions can have on patients. By leveraging our extensive expertise, we ensure that your systems remain reliable, secure, and up-to-date.

  • Proactive First-Line Support

    Addressing immediate user concerns and minor system glitches is crucial in healthcare settings. Our first-line support swiftly resolves these issues, preventing them from escalating into critical problems, thereby ensuring continuous care delivery without interruptions.

  • Dedicated Troubleshooting

    Our troubleshooting team specialises in healthcare systems, identifying and rectifying core issues. We address bugs, perform critical updates, and adjust configurations to maintain the integrity of software, essential for patient data management and daily operations.

  • Advanced Support

    For more complex challenges inherent in healthcare technology, we refine functionality for clinical effectiveness, integrate innovative features for enhanced patient care, and ensure systems meet the highest standards of medical regulatory compliance.

IT Support and Maintenance Roadmap

At IT Medical, our maintenance and support services follow a structured, tried-and-tested framework that enables us to provide you with the best possible service to suit your unique requirements.

  1. System Evaluation

    Initiating with a meticulous audit, we delve into the software’s architecture and coding, alongside a thorough examination of documentation and an assessment of functionality to establish a baseline for improvements.

  2. Maintenance Strategy

    Utilising the insights gained from our initial analysis, we develop a strategic maintenance blueprint, working in partnership with the client to outline a comprehensive scope of support and maintenance services.

  3. Roadmap Formulation

    We proceed to articulate the project scope into a strategic roadmap, meticulously outlining the intended maintenance actions and the selection of appropriate support tools for flowless execution.

  4. Project Transition

    After finalising a maintenance strategy that aligns with the client’s vision, we smoothly transition the project for our specialised team to begin implementation, ensuring continuity and alignment with the agreed-upon plan.

  5. Ongoing Support

    Our team, proficient and well-versed in maintenance disciplines, executes ongoing, proactive support according to the agreed strategy, ensuring readiness to tackle emergent needs promptly.

Maintenance Strategies

We specialise in navigating healthcare organisations through the global shift to cloud technologies, offering strategies for seamless cloud implementation, migration, and integration across public, private, hybrid, and multi-cloud environments.

  • Adaptive

    Through adaptive maintenance, our experts can adjust digital tools and applications to retain functionality and stability in response to substantial changes to software environments.

  • Corrective

    Corrective maintenance involves scheduled or emergency repairs to detect and fix bugs or glitches in software, ensuring high-quality operation and optimal experience.

  • Perfective

    Perfective maintenance involves enhancing digital tools to improve performance and expand lifespan, so you can rest assured that your software will perform optimally for a long time.

  • Preventative

    Through preventative maintenance, our highly skilled maintenance team will augment features of your digital services so you can be sure your software is unsusceptible to faults later in life.

Regulatory and Compliance

In maintaining global compliance, we have fortified our capacity to produce software conforming to international healthcare norms such as HIPAA, IEC 62304, DICOM, ISO 13485, HL7, and FDA 21. Our certification portfolio now includes CISSP, PCI, FIPS 140-2, ISO, GDPR, and HIPAA compliance, in addition to alignment with HL7 International standards.

Service Delivery Models

At IT Medical, we understand that every healthcare organisation is different. That’s why we have a range of service delivery models, allowing organisations to choose the strategy that works best for their unique goals, objectives, and requirements.

  • Managed support

    Our team ensures round-the-clock surveillance of your digital infrastructure's performance and reliability, conducts regular maintenance of all systems and servers, and delivers rapid troubleshooting solutions when necessary for a fixed monthly fee.

  • Pay-as-you-go support

    Our skilled technicians, engineers, and consultants are available to supplement your team, offering their expertise for the duration you require. This model allows you to only pay for the support you use, providing flexibility and efficiency.

  • Emergency support

    Should you face any unforeseen software malfunctions or security breaches, IT Medical’s specialised team is ready to provide prompt and effective support, resolving critical security challenges and system failures, and ensuring minimal disruption to your operations.
