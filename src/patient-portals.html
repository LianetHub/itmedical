Key patient portal solutions capabilities

Patient portals include a range of essential features that support each step of a patient’s medical journey.

  • Access to records

    Authorized patients can access their health examination summaries, lab results, interactions with medical professionals, self-recorded symptoms, wearables, and medical device data stored in the organization’s database.

  • Communication

    Patients with an internet connection can contact their care team in real-time without going to the doctor’s office to consult about non-urgent issues or clarify healthcare-related questions.

  • Patient self-service

    Patients or their representatives can schedule appointments, online consultations, or prescription refills, contact customer support, enter their health data, and sign up for a clinical trial program.

  • Patient Education

    A patient portal can be an educational hub that promotes a healthy lifestyle, helps patients understand their conditions and treatment better, and alerts them of potential health risks.

  • Feedback aggregation

    Patients and care providers can complete forms via patient portals to evaluate the quality of the organization’s care and customer services, work conditions, and other parameters.

  • Insurance management

    With the help of payment system integration, patients can view and manage their insurance claims and payments for each medical event.
