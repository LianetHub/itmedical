Our Telehealth Software Development Services

Telehealth software enables healthcare professionals to remotely provide clinical and non-clinical services, including examination, diagnostics, and treatment prescription, as well as consultations, medical education, and training, via telecommunication technology.

  • Consulting

    Our consultancy service is dedicated to helping healthcare providers decipher their strategic objectives and translate them into actionable technology requirements for telehealth. We help ensure seamless and secure implementation that enhances patient care.

  • Development

    Our engineers excel in developing bespoke telehealth software, from concept through to completion. We focus on crafting user-centric digital tools that fit healthcare providers' unique workflow needs and improve patient engagement and treatment delivery.

  • Integration

    Our integration services ensure that your telehealth platforms communicate flawlessly with existing healthcare ecosystems. From database consolidation to app data synchronisation, we enable a unified health IT environment, facilitating a holistic view of patient health.

  • Modernisation

    We transform outdated healthcare applications into modern, secure, and regulatory-compliant telehealth systems. By revamping legacy software, we align your technology stack with current healthcare trends, ensuring a seamless experience for providers & patients.

  • Compliance

    Navigating the complex regulatory environment of telehealth, we ensure all solutions are compliant with standards such as HIPAA, GDPR, and HITECH. Our compliance updates keep your telehealth services in line with the latest protocols, safeguarding sensitive data.

  • QA & Testing

    Quality assurance and thorough testing underpin our telehealth solutions, ensuring reliability and security. From routine checks to rigorous stress testing, our QA processes verify that your telehealth platforms deliver exceptional performance and user experience.

Our telemedicine solutions

Our experts help choose, design, configure or customize, implement, integrate, and upgrade telehealth software for any organization, meeting the unique requirements of the medical field.

  • We deliver telehealth solutions integrated with EHR, practice management software, and third-party apps to enable MDs, DOs, nurses, and physician assistants to provide preventive, diagnostic, and curative medical services remotely with the help of synchronous and asynchronous communication tools.

  • Our telehealth app development team creates solutions for dermatologists to perform diagnostics from anywhere at any time, using the tools for secure audio/video consultations, image sharing, remote patient monitoring, and secure messaging.

  • We enhance the interoperability of all of your healthcare solutions by properly integrating them. Our team can perform data migration from a legacy hospital management software solution to a new one to ensure patient record safety and correctness.

  • Our solutions facilitate communication between neurology professionals, their colleagues, patients, and caregivers, allowing the fast provision of remote neurological and psychiatric consultations, diagnostics, recommendations, medication e-prescriptions, and non-invasive treatments.

  • We develop custom telehealth apps based on the requirements specific to the particular practice, update or completely rework legacy software, and integrate new telemedicine solutions into the established medical IT ecosystem to boost the healthcare organization’s efficiency.
