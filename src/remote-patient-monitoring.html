Benefits of a remote patient monitoring
(RPM) software

Remote patient monitoring systems have valuable advantages for patients, medical personnel, and healthcare organizations.

  • For Patients
    • Can access their healthcare team anytime
    • Don’t have to travel to the doctor’s office
    • Experience better health outcomes
    • Have more control over their condition
  • For professionals
    • Better understanding of patients’ conditions
    • Improve the quality of patient care
    • Can dedicate more time to critical patients
    • Can intervene before the condition worsens
  • For organisations
    • Reduce patient care expenses
    • Improve medical staff efficiency
    • Enhance patient engagement
    • Increase total revenue

Main features of RPM software

The major purpose of RPM software is to collect data on patients’ vital metrics and deliver it to medical professionals. However, modern patient monitoring platforms enable a much more robust functionality.

    • Compare patients’ vitals against reference parameters
    • Identify trends in symptom changes
    • Chart history of patients’ vitals
    • Generate comprehensive reports
    • Interview patients and log symptoms using chatbots
    • Alert designated people of any abnormalities
    • Supply patients with condition management support
    • Answer common non-urgent questions
    • Make devices controllable by voice
    • Add text-to-speech functionality
    • Let users switch to a larger font
    • Provide a high-contrast app design option
    • Encrypt all data passing through RPM devices
    • Ensure compliance with HIPAA and other regulations
    • Assign different access profiles for various groups of users
    • Enable multi-factor user authentication
    • Let designated personnel manage RPM parameters
    • Identify signs of a device not functioning properly
    • Alert users about possible device failure
    • Block all device operations in case of a malfunction

Valuable RPM integrations

While RPM solutions alone greatly benefit healthcare organizations and patients, their full potential is unlocked in an integrated healthcare IT ecosystem.

Valuable RPM integrations
  • EHR/EMR systems
  • Healthcare IoT
  • Telehealth apps
  • Patient portals
  • Mobile health apps
  • Healthcare data analytic solutions
  • EHR/EMR systems
    Make sure that patients’ medical history is continuously updated with new information from their RPM devices so that medical personnel have a complete up-to-date view of the patients’ conditions.
  • Healthcare IoT
    Expand your digital care services by enabling IoMT devices like drug delivery gadgets and virtual ward systems to take action automatically based on the readings from integrated RPM devices.
  • Telehealth apps
    Equip your specialists with tools for remote health examination during their teleconsultation. With connected RPM devices, a virtual visit is just as effective in terms of diagnostic and care quality as an in-person one.
  • Patient portals
    Enable healthcare professionals to consult, diagnose, and prescribe treatments to patients remotely using various secure communication tools, remote monitoring medical devices, and mHealth apps.
  • Mobile health apps
    Allow medical personnel and patients to have access to RPM readings, log symptoms and other vital information, and exchange such data with other systems on the go, by integrating RPM devices with mHealth applications.
  • Healthcare data analytic solutions
    Transform data received from RPM devices into the basis for accurate diagnosis and personalized treatment plans by finding meaningful patterns in patients’ condition changes, symptoms, and vitals with the help of medical analytic solutions.
