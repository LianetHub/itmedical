Our Hospital Management Software (HMS) Services

Hospital management software (HMS) streamlines healthcare organizations’ clinical and administrative workflows by centralizing data, coordinating and automating clinical, business, patient, and facility management, and serving as a cross-department platform for decision-making and collaboration.

  • Operational & tactical solutions

    We build software systems for data management across the hospital capable of processing patient, laboratory, pharmacy, and equipment information, as well as assisting with facility operation and evidence-based decision-making.

  • Patient relations management

    IT Medical creates web and mobile solutions that facilitate real-time and asynchronous interactions with patients, enables patient self-management, and allows healthcare providers to enhance patient experience, boost loyalty, and improve outcomes.

  • Profile-based solutions

    We develop secure EHR/EMR, CRM, LIMS, and other systems to collect and store complete patient information, allowing healthcare providers to streamline care coordination based on 360-degree profiles.

  • Task-based solutions

    IT Medical can deliver HMS, HRM, and practice management software that will help to properly distribute the workload for designated hospital staff and then assist them with performing their day-to-day tasks and responsibilities faster and error-free.

  • Billing & claims processing

    We build financial management systems that help streamline invoicing and insurance collection, manage patient revenue cycles, and keep track of the cash flow, as well as flawlessly integrate with e-payment, claims management, and insurance providers’ platforms.

  • Clinical Decision Support

    We develop advanced analytics platforms that assist healthcare professionals in making informed decisions. These systems integrate real-time patient data with the latest medical research to provide evidence-based recommendations, enhancing the quality of care and patient outcomes.

Hospital Management Software Benefits

  • Automation

    HMS digitizes paper-based and manual hospital workflows, improving data processing efficiency and minimizing human errors.

  • Cost-efficiency

    By optimizing operational workflows and providing visibility into inventory, HMS enhances overall hospital performance, resource management, and cost allocation.

  • Improved patient care

    HMS ensures patient data accuracy and completeness, allowing physicians to deliver more personalized treatment and preventive care based on patient data trends.

  • Regulatory compliance

    A sophisticated HMS operates in line with data security and privacy guidelines so that hospitals can protect patient data and qualify for Meaningful Use requirements.
